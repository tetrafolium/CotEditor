CotEditor crashes

If you get frequent crashes of CotEditor under certain circumstances, please send a crash log on the following page:

  • Issues coteditor/CotEditor -github

When you send a crash log, don't forget to include the following information:

  • The operation you performed just before the crash.
  • Your environment information such as the version of macOS, the version of CotEditor, your machine model, and so on.

You can display a crash log by clicking Report in the dialog which appears on the crash.
