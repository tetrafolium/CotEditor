Changing the range of possible completions

You can expand/limit the range of possible completions. The completion list can show only the words in your document, or additionally, the words specified by your syntax style, or along with them all, standard words.

  1. Choose CotEditor > Preferences.
  2. Select Edit pane.
  3. Under Completion List, select the range of your choice.
